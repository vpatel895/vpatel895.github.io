Overview

A cricket bat is a specialized piece of equipment used in the sport of cricket. It is typically made of wood and consists of a long handle connected to a flat blade. The blade is used to strike the ball during the game.

Features

  • Wooden construction for durability
  • Handle wrapped with rubber grip for better control
  • Flat blade with a slight curve for power and accuracy
  • Weight distribution designed for balance and maneuverability
